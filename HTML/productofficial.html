Juniper Bonsai


Juniper bonsai are the most popular bonsai trees since it is easy to maintain and care.Their branches are flexible and easy to shape causing the planter to style it as he/she likes. They will thrive in many type of climates as well. They have evergreen, needle-like foliage that ranges from dark green to steely blue. Most juniper varietals will grow at a steady pace. It will grow about 28-30 inches long.
